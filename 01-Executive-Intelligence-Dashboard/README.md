Project 1: Executive Intelligence Dashboard
The Problem: High-stakes decision-making often relies on slow, manual research that can miss critical "red flags" or emerging market shifts.

The Solution: An AI-driven research pipeline that synthesizes 500+ real-time sources into a clinical investment memo.

The Methodology: Leveraged Perplexity Pro for deep-crawling market data and Gemini 3.1 Pro for strategic synthesis.

The Edge: Implemented a "Skeptical VC" persona to stress-test data and identify regulatory risks that standard AI summaries often overlook.
