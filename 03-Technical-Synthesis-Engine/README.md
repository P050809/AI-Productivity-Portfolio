Project 3: Technical Synthesis Engine
The Problem: Manually auditing 200+ page technical or legal documents is time-consuming and prone to human error, especially when cross-referencing distant sections.

The Solution: A high-context "Policy Q&A" assistant capable of handling massive datasets with zero-shot accuracy.

The Methodology: Deployed Gemini 3.1 Pro with a 2-million-token context window at Temperature 0.0 for maximum factual reliability.

The Edge: Provides 100% verifiable citations, including exact page numbers and section titles, for every technical claim made.
