Project 2: Sequential Marketing Suite
The Problem: Automated marketing content frequently suffers from "brand drift," where the tone and strategy become inconsistent across different platforms.

The Solution: A specialized multi-agent workflow that automates the journey from a raw idea to a full, brand-consistent campaign in minutes.

The Methodology: Utilized a "Manager-Node" prompting architecture in ChatGPT to coordinate between three distinct personas: Strategist, Copywriter, and Creative Director.

The Edge: Ensures 100% alignment between the high-level brand strategy and the final creative assets (ads, emails, and social posts).
