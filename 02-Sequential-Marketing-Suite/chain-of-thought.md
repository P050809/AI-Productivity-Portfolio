🛠️ Phase 1: The "Master Multi-Agent" Prompt
Instead of making three bots, you will create a "System Command" that tells ChatGPT to act as a project manager for three internal agents.

Copy and use this as your first message to ChatGPT:

"Act as an AI Marketing Manager. I want you to run a multi-agent workflow for a project. You have three internal specialists:

The Strategist: Defines ICP, Brand Voice, and USP.

The Copywriter: Creates LinkedIn posts and cold emails based on the Strategist’s output.

The Creative Director: Generates DALL-E image prompts for ads.

Workflow Rule: You will only process one agent at a time. After each agent finishes, ask me for 'Approval' before moving to the next specialist.

The Project: [Insert your idea here, e.g., A gym supplement for busy programmers].

Start now with The Strategist."

📂 Phase 2: The "Zero-Cost" Execution
Step 1: The AI will give you the strategy. Read it, and type "Approved. Move to the Copywriter."

Step 2: The AI will now write the posts/emails. Type "Approved. Move to the Creative Director."

Step 3: The AI will give you the image prompts.

The Result: You now have a single, long chat thread that looks like a professional agency hand-off.
